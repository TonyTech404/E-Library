Atomic habits

"Atomic Habits: An Easy & Proven Way to Build Good Habits & Break Bad Ones" is a transformative self-help book written by James Clear, a leading expert on habit formation. Published in 2018, the book rapidly became a bestseller due to its practical and evidence-based approach to building positive habits and breaking those that are detrimental.

James Clear distills complex behavioral psychology into an accessible guide that empowers readers to master the tiny behaviors that lead to remarkable results. He posits that success doesn’t come from major, isolated acts, but from the small, incremental decisions we make every day. The book's central idea is that making tiny changes will yield extraordinarily consistent and compound growth over time, hence the title "Atomic Habits."

Throughout "Atomic Habits," Clear supports his insights with a blend of scientific research, personal stories, and real-world examples, which illustrate how these principles can be applied to every aspect of life. Whether the goal is to excel in business, improve health, or master an art, his techniques show that small adjustments can lead to new identity-based habits, where the focus shifts from what you want to achieve to who you wish to become.
